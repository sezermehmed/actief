Prevent fire in your company

Fires occur in a large number of companies every year. At least one in ten industrial fires is caused by negligence during fire-hazardous work. Arson and unsafe use of the electrical installation are also common causes of fire. Many companies that are affected by a major fire do not recover from the consequences. Good fire insurance is therefore a must, but it is even better to prevent fire in your business premises. Fire can occur in various ways, for example due to incendiary work or arson.
