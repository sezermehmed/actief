First aid kits



In the Netherlands, an average of 280,000 people per year sustain injuries as a result of an industrial accident. The presence of good company first aid kits is essential. The Oranje Kruis, the organization that provides first aid diplomas and compiles teaching materials, has drawn up guidelines for the contents of the first aid kit. The basic tasks of the emergency responder have been taken into account. The basic principle is that the first-aid kit is effective, safe, efficient and user-friendly. According to the Orange Cross, the suitcase should be reachable within half a minute. As a result, more suitcases may be needed in a company. These recommendations supplement the minimum provisions in the Working Conditions Act.
